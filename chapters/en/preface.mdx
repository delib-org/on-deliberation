# Preface

*On Deliberation* is structured as a living argument rather than a frozen manuscript. The repository keeps the writing open to critique, reinforcement, translation, and formal refinement without losing citation quality or editorial accountability.

This work should be read as a theoretical framework under active development. Some claims are intentionally presented as hypotheses that invite stronger tests, clearer counterexamples, and better operational definitions. That stance follows the critical tradition associated with [@popper1959].

Readers can move from this preface to the [Introduction](/en/introduction), open a page-scoped issue through the feedback panel, or inspect the canonical [References](/en/references).

## Working Norm

When you propose a change, treat it as a refinement of a specific argument rather than a global reaction. That makes disagreement easier to evaluate, easier to merge, and easier to cite later on.
